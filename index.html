How to Play

Covid-19 has put a wrench in all of our plans, and I know I haven't been playing magic with you guys as much as I would've liked to. So I put together this website, and using untap.in we can all play magic the gathering(almost)like we used to.


untap.in is a great site(and app!) where we can all play mtg for free! It has all of the cards available for deckbuilding, including custom cards and alternate arts.


So log-in /create an account, build your deck and join in the fun every Monday @3:30 pm PT on Untap and zoom
