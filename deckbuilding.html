Deckbuilding with Untap

To start your first deck or a new one, go to untap.in and select the decks tab, from there you can create a new deck, or edit an existing deck. Make sure to select "MTG" and give your deck a name. The bar on the left will let you search for cards, or select paste deck from the hamburger menu to import a decklist.


You can also select download deck and follow those instructions which can be used to create a .txt file that you can copy and paste all of the cards into a comment on the document below to share your deck with others.

Adjusting Power level

Throughout our club power level of decks has increasingly become a concern. In order to promote a more inclusive space to play magic I propose the following deckbuilding limitations: -20$ limit on commanders -10$ limit on each individual card in a deck -Proxies are allowed as long as the original cards fit the budget limits< -Silver-boardered cards and acorn stamped cards are allowed to help promote funny decks over powerful ones -Lets play some other formats too, I'd like to play a game of oathbreaker or 2-headed dragon as these formats use similar decks with different rules -limit 1 10 cycle infinite combo/game -10 min turn limit
